# Strong Action Verbs for ATS-Optimized Resumes

Use these to replace weak verbs like "helped", "worked on", "responsible for", "assisted". Choose based on context and JD language. Always pair with specific results.

## Leadership & Management
- Directed, Led, Spearheaded, Orchestrated, Championed, Headed, Oversaw, Managed, Supervised, Coordinated, Facilitated, Mentored, Coached, Guided, Inspired, Motivated, Empowered, Delegated, Aligned, Mobilized

## Strategy & Planning
- Strategized, Planned, Designed, Developed, Formulated, Architected, Engineered, Established, Founded, Launched, Initiated, Pioneered, Conceptualized, Outlined, Devised, Mapped, Forecasted, Projected, Anticipated

## Execution & Delivery
- Executed, Delivered, Implemented, Deployed, Rolled out, Operationalized, Produced, Generated, Completed, Achieved, Attained, Secured, Obtained, Acquired, Fulfilled, Realized, Accomplished, Finalized

## Improvement & Optimization
- Optimized, Improved, Enhanced, Upgraded, Streamlined, Simplified, Automated, Digitized, Modernized, Revamped, Transformed, Reengineered, Redesigned, Restructured, Consolidated, Centralized, Standardized, Benchmarked, Elevated, Maximized, Minimized, Reduced, Cut, Decreased, Increased, Boosted, Accelerated, Expedited, Amplified, Strengthened, Fortified

## Analysis & Problem-Solving
- Analyzed, Evaluated, Assessed, Diagnosed, Investigated, Researched, Audited, Reviewed, Examined, Scrutinized, Identified, Uncovered, Detected, Resolved, Troubleshot, Debugged, Solved, Mitigated, Alleviated, Rectified, Remediated, Diagnosed

## Collaboration & Communication
- Collaborated, Partnered, Co-created, Facilitated, Mediated, Negotiated, Influenced, Persuaded, Presented, Communicated, Articulated, Advocated, Championed, Liaised, Engaged, Consulted, Advised, Counseled, Trained, Educated, Mentored, Onboarded, Aligned, Synthesized, Translated, Bridged

## Technical & Creation (esp. for IT/AI/Engineering)
- Built, Constructed, Developed, Engineered, Programmed, Coded, Architected, Designed, Created, Generated, Produced, Fabricated, Assembled, Integrated, Configured, Deployed, Implemented, Automated, Scripted, Modeled, Simulated, Trained (models), Fine-tuned, Optimized (algorithms), Scaled, Debugged, Refactored, Migrated, Upgraded, Customized, Extended, Enhanced

## Growth & Business Impact
- Grew, Expanded, Scaled, Accelerated, Drove, Generated, Produced, Delivered, Captured, Acquired, Retained, Increased (revenue, users, efficiency), Reduced (costs, churn, time, errors), Saved, Avoided, Prevented, Mitigated risk, Enabled, Supported, Facilitated (X% growth), Contributed to, Powered, Fueled

## Quantification Helpers (pair with verbs)
- By X% / X-fold / from X to Y
- Generating $X in revenue / savings / cost avoidance
- Serving / impacting / supporting X users / customers / stakeholders / team members
- Across X teams / departments / regions / countries
- Within X months / weeks / days (time-to-value)
- Reducing X (time, steps, errors, tickets) by Y%
- Increasing X (throughput, accuracy, satisfaction, adoption) by Y%

## Tips for Use
- Match verb tense to role: Past for previous positions, present for current.
- Vary verbs across bullets – avoid repetition.
- Choose verbs that appear in or align with JD responsibilities (e.g., if JD says "drive cross-functional initiatives", use "Orchestrated cross-functional..." or "Led cross-functional...").
- For senior roles: Favor strategic verbs (Architected, Spearheaded, Orchestrated) over tactical.
- For individual contributor/tech: Balance with creation/optimization verbs (Built, Optimized, Automated, Scaled).
